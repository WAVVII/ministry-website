Ministry Of Health And Human Services

The Ministry

The Ministry of Health and Human Services has the cabinet responsibility of implementing national and state government health policies as well as providing technical input to assist Benue State government in the formulation of its health policies for execution. This organisation is set at ensuring the 100% health stability of Benue State.
